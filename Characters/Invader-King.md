## Personality

[WIP] — Exercises power as a natural condition rather than a pursuit. Keeping subjugated species in his ranks is not sentiment; it is a demonstration. The Martian arrangement is a necessary inconvenience he has never pretended to enjoy.

## Role in the Conflict

The Invader King's relationship with Earth's conquest is strategic and unhurried. He does not need the planet today. What he needs is a coalition that remains functional while the problem is worked out — and the Martians are, currently, the best instrument available for working it out.

He is aware that the Martians are scheming. He has been aware of this for some time. His calculation is that the cost of eliminating them exceeds the cost of managing them. This calculation has not yet been proven wrong.
