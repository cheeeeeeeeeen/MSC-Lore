## Origin & History

[WIP] — Rose under Donald Morden's leadership as a direct challenge to the established world order and, by extension, the Regular Army. At the time of Martian first contact, it was the most organized and militarily capable insurgent force on Earth.

### The Martian Alliance

The Rebel Army entered a technology-exchange arrangement with Martian agents prior to the current conflict. The Martians wanted intelligence and an Earth foothold; the Rebel Army wanted technology. Both sides were using the other. Morden detected signs of an incoming Martian betrayal — they had done it before — and was already preparing to cut the arrangement when the Professor's involvement accelerated the collapse. The separation was earlier and messier than either party planned.

The technology exchanged during the alliance exists on both sides. The Rebel Army retains what it learned. The Martians know exactly what the Rebel Army walked away with. Neither the Rebel Army nor the Martians have fully disclosed this to their respective current partners.
